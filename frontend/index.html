The Process

Three Steps to a Credit Decision

No branch visits. No stacks of paper. No waiting weeks. RinSathi uses AI to assess your loan application the moment you submit it.

1
🪪
Verify Your Identity
Enter your National Identity Number. RinSathi verifies it instantly against Nepal's DoNIDCR national registry and retrieves your land assets from NeLIS — no document scanning required.
2
🤖
AI Assesses Your Profile
Our five-agent pipeline analyzes your income sources, land assets, and repayment capacity using XGBoost machine learning. Every decision is fully explainable.
3
✅
Officer Reviews and Decides
A qualified loan officer reviews the AI assessment and makes the final decision. You are notified on your dashboard with the officer's written justification.
Why RinSathi

Built for Nepal's Rural Reality

Designed from the ground up for Nepal's microfinance context — not adapted from a foreign system.

🪪
NIN-Based KYC
Identity verified directly against DoNIDCR — the same source banks use. No document forgery risk. No OCR errors. Authoritative verification from Nepal's government registry.
🌾
Land Asset Verification
Land ownership automatically retrieved from NeLIS using your citizenship number. No need to bring your Lalpurja. Your assets are verified from the land registry directly.
📱
Digital Income Sources
eSewa, Khalti, remittances, and cooperative savings all count as income signals. No formal salary slip required. Rural and informal incomes are recognized and scored fairly.
🔍
Explainable AI
Every credit score comes with a plain-language explanation using SHAP values. The officer and applicant can see exactly which factors drove the decision — no black box.
👨‍💼
Human-in-the-Loop
AI recommends. Officers decide. Every loan decision is made by a qualified human who reviews the AI's full reasoning before approving or rejecting.
📋
Full Audit Trail
Every agent decision, officer action, and system event is logged with timestamps. Complete traceability for NRB regulatory inspection at any time.
